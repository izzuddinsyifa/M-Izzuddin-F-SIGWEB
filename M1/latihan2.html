Orcinus orca (Killer Whale)

Orcinus orca, commonly known as the killer whale, is a highly intelligent apex predator. It exhibits complex social structures within pods and employs sophisticated hunting strategies. Found in diverse marine environments worldwide, orcas are known for their distinctive black and white coloration and vocal communication abilities.
